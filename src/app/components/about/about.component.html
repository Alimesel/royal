Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 1998, Royal Taste has been at the forefront of luxury dining, nestled within the iconic Grand Royal Hotel. Our kitchen is a stage where passion meets precision, and every dish is a testament to decades of culinary mastery.

We source ingredients from certified organic farms and the world's most prestigious suppliers — Wagyu from Japan, truffles from Périgord, saffron from Kashmir. Every plate tells a story of origin, craft, and devotion.

Our team of 32 culinary artists bring diverse expertise, united by one philosophy: dining should nourish not just the body, but the soul.
